The Dragon Prince


The sky was dyed with a bright red color, the smell of blood filled the cold wind that night, there was fog mixed with smoke, and ashes floating all around the place. Foes and allies fighting one another. Reiko was there, unable to identity between the two; it was all lost. “How could the peace that had reigned for centuries be shattered so easily?, Everything was normal a few months ago, so why did it had to come to this?” That were Reiko’s thoughts as she drew her weapon and approached the core of the endless battle. Reiko was about to fight her dearest and most precious friends….
